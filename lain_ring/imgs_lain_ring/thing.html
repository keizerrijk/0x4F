My Criteria

In general I don't actively take part in their development and they don't necessarily reflect my views.

I may on infrequent occasion exclude some sites:

  • It is behind Cloudflare
  • Non-personal (e.g. an imageboard) sites which have not added the ring locally
  • Sites which lose most or all functionality without JavaScript
  • My own standards for objectionable content (e.g. porn on the front page)

I don't by any means control the web ring, these are just my personal criteria. Sites I exclude may be included in other webmaster's local copies, at their discretion.

Those under "Tor" do not have a clearnet mirror, those under "Clearnet" may or may not have other mirrors.
